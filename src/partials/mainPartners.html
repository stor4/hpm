Motor Sich JSC

Motor Sich Company was founded on May 31, 1946. The enterprise was headed by eminent aircraft designer Oleg Antonov.
During this time more than one hundred types and modifications of passenger, transport and special purpose airplanes
were designed by ANTONOV team. More than 22 000 such aircraft were constructed.
